Welcome to WorldTore Travels

After working with many travel agencies, tour providers, and travel websites over the years, we’ve learnt a fair bit about these kinds of websites. One thing has become extremely clear to us: travel agency website designs are possibly the most important part of driving conversions. Let’s not get it twisted – content is king, and the content / tours / travel options provided are almost always the most important factor on the site. But if you take two sites with the same level of content quality, it’s no doubt that it’ll be the travel websites’ designs that become the deciding factor for the customer, and the usability of the website that drives conversions and directs traffic. We’ve used our experience with these kinds of websites to write this guide on the best tour and travel website designs. Finding the best practices and design trends that will help you turn visits into conversions, and possibly even repeat customers. Let’s take a closer look.
